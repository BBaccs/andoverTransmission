About Wayne

Wayne Grenewicz's shop has been servicing the Newton area since 2003, a shop run by Wayne and his wife, has survived 15+ years of services virtually on word of mouth alone. The Newton native prides himself on honest evaluations, and quality work. His reputation truly precedes him. Wayne is teh father of one boy and one girl, both who can be seen learning the ins and outs of the shops speciality, transmission rebuilding.
